The AI Engine
How similarity learning powers every recommendation
  • TF-IDF Vectorization

    Course tags, categories and descriptions are converted into weighted term-frequency vectors — capturing what each course is truly about.

  • Profile Vector Construction

    Your interests are embedded in the same vector space as the courses, creating a mathematical representation of your learning DNA.

  • Cosine Similarity Matching

    The angle between your profile vector and each course vector determines relevance. Closer angle = better match, regardless of vector magnitude.

  • Blended Scoring

    Final score = 80% cosine similarity + 20% course rating, adjusted by a difficulty preference multiplier for precision matching.
